RunAtive team

Runative is an international company with offices in Barcelona (Spain) and Limassol (Cyprus). We are always looking for new ambitious and talented people, who, just like us, are driven by the desire to change the advertising landscape for the better. We believe work & life go hand in hand and thrive on making sure we enjoy both to the fullest!

Our company benefits

Our Amazing Offices

All our offices are brand new, easy to get to, bright and fully stocked with snacks and fresh fruit and drinks. We do everything for you to feel at home.

Private Health Care

We want our teammates to stay fit and healthy! Thanks to our private health care and dental plan you are fully taken care of from the very first day you start!

Personal and Professional Growth

We care about our teammates like we care aboutour friends. We are here to help you progress in any personal or professional growth plans you would like to achieve.

Flexibility

Having some troubles in your apartment ? Need to visit the doctor or dentist or have a bank appointment ? Working hours at RUNative are flexible and we don’t micro-manage people. We only ask results and ambition in return.

Team Events

We like to work hard and play hard. Every year we t ake the whole team out on a one week trip somewhere nice. During the year you can also expect multiple events or team nights out. Having fun after work is something we value a lot.

Right tools for the job

We can’t be a tech company without offering our employees the latest tech toys. New macbook or windows laptop, smartphone with international phone subscription or anything else you need to do your job ? We’ve got you covered!

Free Language Classes

Don’t speak Spanish? Only English is mandatory to be part of RUNative, but we offer free language classes for anyone who joins us!

Tradeshows around the world

We go out and put our own name on the map! Join RUNative and represent the team at various tradeshows around the world!

Happy Hours for everyone

We are bringing happy hours to the office, with our recently installed beer-tap you will have unlimited access to beer and many other drinks after working hours.
